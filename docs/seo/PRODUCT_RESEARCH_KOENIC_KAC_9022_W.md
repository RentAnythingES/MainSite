## Operational Copy Constraints

- This is a portable monobloc air conditioner, not a permanently installed split system.
- Cooling requires the exhaust hose to vent warm air outdoors through a suitable window or opening.
- Actual cooling performance depends on room volume, insulation, sun exposure, occupancy, and hose/window setup.
- At 29.5 kg, delivery and placement must be agreed; customers should not be encouraged to carry it alone.
- Do not promise silent operation. The declared sound power is 62 dB(A).
- Do not claim WLAN/app control for the base `KAC 9022 W` listing unless the physical unit is confirmed as the WLAN variant.
