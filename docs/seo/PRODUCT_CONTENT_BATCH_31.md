## Verification Notes

- The kayak detailed description states a 245 kg capacity while the specification table states 240 kg. The draft uses the lower 240 kg limit.
- The kayak supplier pack does not include a pump or paddles. These must be configured as explicit rental-package components before activation.
- The paddleboard's 290 kg figure is maximum flotation load, not the recommended standing-user limit. The draft uses the 130 kg user design limit prominently.
- Both supplier pages restrict navigation to within 300 metres of a place of refuge.
- Primary supplier images were downloaded into Supabase Storage; image rights remain `unknown` pending approval.
