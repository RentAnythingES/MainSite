## Operational Constraints

- This is a portable monobloc unit and requires the warm-air hose to vent outdoors.
- Cooling performance depends on room size, insulation, direct sun, occupancy, and the quality of the window seal.
- Do not promise silent operation; portable compressors remain audible even at lower speed.
- Confirm a suitable window/opening, power socket, delivery access, and placement before handover.
- Keep the appliance upright and never open or modify the R290 refrigerant circuit.
