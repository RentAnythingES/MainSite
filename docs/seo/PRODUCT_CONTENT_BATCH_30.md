## Verification Notes

- The AQUASTIC listing shows restraint straps but does not expose a child-use certification, age range, reliable dimensions or numeric load limit. The draft permits equipment transport only until the exact manual is reviewed.
- The Aktive wagon has a supplier-listed 100 kg load, removable tabletop, removable washable polyester body and two 360° wheels with brakes. It is recorded as equipment-only.
- The Deuter feature copy gives a carrier weight of 3.23 kg, while one supplier specification field appears to omit a decimal and displays 323 g. The draft uses 3.23 kg and records the conflict for physical verification.
- The INTEX feature summary contains a truncated `52 × 236 cm` dimension, but its detailed description and width specification state 152 × 236 cm. The draft uses the detailed 152 × 236 × 46 cm dimensions.
- Decathlon spells the Hamax model `Pionner`. The current Hamax catalogue does not confirm this model name, so the source spelling is retained and exact model/manual identification is a publication gate.
- Supplier images are copied into Supabase Storage rather than hotlinked. Image rights remain `unknown` until approved in the admin workflow.
