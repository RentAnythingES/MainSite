# Partnership Authority Surface

**Implemented:** 19 July 2026
**English owner:** `/partners`
**Spanish equivalent:** `/es/colaboraciones`

## Purpose

The partnership surface is a business-development and backlink destination for
accommodation operators, travel and relocation services, product brands,
distributors, publications, and creators. It is not a consumer rental landing
page and must not compete with category, product, kit, or Host Services intent.

The page gives legitimate prospective partners a stable URL to evaluate and link
to. Its conversion goal is a specific partnership enquiry through the existing
Resend-backed contact system.

## Intent ownership

| Intent | Owner |
|---|---|
| Rent equipment in Valencia | Category and product pages |
| Configure a multi-item rental | `/valencia/kits` |
| Arrange equipment for accommodation guests | `/valencia/host-services` |
| Discuss referrals, operational collaboration, or product pilots | `/partners` |

## Supported partnership models

1. Direct guest referrals from accommodation providers.
2. Defined operational support for recurring accommodation needs.
3. Narrow product pilots with two to five relevant products and an agreed use case.
4. Transparent editorial collaboration that serves a real travel-planning need.

## Publication guardrails

- Do not show logos until a relationship exists and brand use is approved.
- Do not imply endorsement, exclusivity, customer proof, or commercial results.
- Do not publish performance metrics before a measurement method and evidence exist.
- Do not share customer identity or contact data in partner reporting.
- Share or publish customer feedback only under the applicable consent.
- Label commercial relationships clearly wherever they affect content or links.
- Create case-study pages only after a completed pilot has reviewable evidence.

## Internal linking

- The global footer links to the localized partnership page.
- The page links to Host Services and Kits to route operational and commercial
  visitors to the correct owner.
- The language switch maps the English and Spanish slugs explicitly.
- Both localized URLs are included in the sitemap with monthly change frequency.
