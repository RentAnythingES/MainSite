# RentAnything.es — Product Sense
> Status: 🔲 Scaffold — to be populated

## Mission
Make renting equipment in Valencia effortless for tourists, families, and digital nomads.

## Target Audiences
1. **Families with young children** — Strollers, cribs, car seats, high chairs
2. **Elderly visitors / mobility needs** — Wheelchairs, scooters, walkers
3. **Digital nomads / remote workers** — Monitors, standing desks, ergonomic chairs
4. **Holiday-makers** — Beach gear, portable AC, air purifiers

## Core Value Props (rank order)
1. **Convenience** — Delivered to your door, same-day available
2. **Trust** — Clean, well-maintained, premium brands
3. **Savings** — Rent vs buy, especially for short stays
4. **Local expertise** — We know Valencia, we'll help you plan

## Brand Voice
**Friendly + Trustworthy.** We talk to families, elderly, and nomads — people who want a caring, helpful service that goes the extra mile. Not corporate. Not salesy. Like a knowledgeable friend who lives in Valencia.
